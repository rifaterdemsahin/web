# Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) for the Website

This document outlines the high-level objectives and measurable key results for the personal brand and business website of Rifat Erdem Sahin.

---

## Objective 1: Establish Rifat Erdem Sahin as a Thought Leader in the AI and DevOps Space

**Description:** This objective focuses on building authority and credibility in the fields of AI solutions architecture and DevOps through valuable content and community engagement.

**Key Results:**

*   **KR1: Increase Newsletter Subscribers:** Grow the newsletter subscriber base by 20% in Q1 2026.
*   **KR2: Consistent High-Quality Content:** Publish at least 4 high-quality, in-depth blog posts per month on relevant AI and DevOps topics.
*   **KR3: Increase Content Engagement:** Achieve a 15% quarter-over-quarter increase in organic traffic to the `/pages/blog.html` and `/pages/courses.html` sections.
*   **KR4: Grow Social Proof:** Secure 5 speaking engagements or podcast interviews in 2026.

---

## Objective 2: Drive Business Growth Through Lead Generation and Course Enrollment

**Description:** This objective is focused on converting website traffic into tangible business opportunities, including consulting leads and course sales.

**Key Results:**

*   **KR1: Generate Qualified Leads:** Generate an average of 50 qualified leads per month through the contact, assessment, and investor forms.
*   **KR2: Increase Ebook Downloads:** Increase downloads of the "RAG Guide" ebook by 25% in Q1 2026.
*   **KR3: Improve Course Funnel Conversion:** Achieve a 10% conversion rate from the early-bird registration list to paid course enrollment for new courses.
*   **KR4: Secure Consulting Engagements:** Convert at least 3 leads per quarter into active consulting engagements.

---

## Objective 3: Enhance User Experience and Engagement on the Website

**Description:** This objective aims to create a seamless, intuitive, and engaging experience for all website visitors, encouraging them to explore the content and services offered.

**Key Results:**

*   **KR1: Improve Website Performance:** Achieve a Google Lighthouse performance score of 90+ for all main pages (`index`, `about`, `contact`, `courses`, `delivery-pilot`).
*   **KR2: Reduce Bounce Rate:** Reduce the overall website bounce rate by 15% by the end of Q2 2026.
*   **KR3: Increase Session Duration:** Increase the average session duration across the website by 20% in Q1 2026.
*   **KR4: Ensure Mobile-First Experience:** Achieve a 100% pass rate on Google's Mobile-Friendly Test for all pages.
