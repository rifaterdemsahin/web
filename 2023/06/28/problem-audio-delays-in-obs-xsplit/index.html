Problem : Audio Delays in OBS/Xsplit

One of the most common issues encountered when streaming is the synchronization of audio and video. This problem can be caused by a variety of factors, such as network latency, buffering, encoding settings, or even the hardware being used. To ensure a smooth streaming experience, it is important to address this issue promptly. One possible solution is to adjust the encoding settings to prioritize video or audio, depending on which is experiencing more latency. Another option is to use a dedicated streaming software that can automatically adjust the synchronization. Additionally, it is recommended to test the stream before going live, and to have a backup plan in case issues arise during the live stream.
