London Trip Itinerary

Date: Wednesday, 10th April 2024

Morning

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at your accommodation in Cambridge.

Departure from Cambridge

  • Train to London: Catch the 9:10 train to London, arriving at Liverpool Street Station.

Mid-Morning

  • Taxi to Hotel: Use Uber or Bolt for a ride to Park Plaza Westminster Bridge London.

Late Morning

  • Tesco for Meal Deals: Visit a nearby Tesco to pick up meal deals for the family.

Late Morning/Early Afternoon

  • Taxi to London Zoo: Use Uber or Bolt for a ride to London Zoo.

Afternoon

  • Exploring London Zoo: Spend time enjoying the zoo's attractions.

Evening

  • Return to Hotel: Use Uber or Bolt to return to Park Plaza Westminster Bridge London around 7:00 PM.

  • Dinner in Hotel Room: Enjoy your Tesco meal deals in your hotel room.

Date: Thursday, Following Day

Morning

  • Breakfast at the Hotel: Start your day with breakfast at the hotel.

  • London Eye and Surroundings: Leave luggage at the hotel and walk around the London Eye area.

Afternoon

  • Return Journey to Cambridge: Catch the 17:00 train back to Cambridge from Liverpool Street Station.

Tips

  • Avoid the Metro: Use ride-sharing services for convenience, especially when traveling with children or luggage.

  • Use Uber and Bolt: Flexible and easy for family travel.

  • Meal Planning: Tesco meal deals are convenient and allow more exploration time.

Formatting Tips for Google Docs:

  • Headings: Use the heading feature for dates and main parts of the day (Morning, Afternoon, Evening). This will help organize the itinerary clearly.

  • Bullets and Numbers: Use bullet points for activities and tips to make the document easy to scan.

  • Bold Text: Consider using bold text for emphasis on certain words or sections, like "Breakfast" or "Train to London."

  • Links: If you have reservations or tickets online, you can insert hyperlinks directly to them in your document.

  • Comments: Utilize the comment feature in Google Docs to add any notes or reminders for yourself about each activity.

  • Tables: If you want to include timings next to each activity, consider using a table with two columns: one for the time and one for the activity.

This structured approach will make your itinerary easy to follow and visually appealing, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable trip.
