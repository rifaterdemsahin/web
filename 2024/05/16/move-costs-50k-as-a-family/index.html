Move costs 50k as a family

The cost of moving from Cambridge, UK, to Vancouver, Canada, can vary widely depending on several factors. Here’s a breakdown of potential costs to give you a rough estimate:

1. Visa and Immigration Fees

  • Express Entry Application Fee: CAD 1,325 (approx. GBP 780) per adult

  • Work Permit Fee: CAD 155 (approx. GBP 90)

  • Medical Examination: CAD 150-300 (approx. GBP 90-180) per person

  • Biometrics Fee: CAD 85 (approx. GBP 50) per person

2. Travel Expenses

  • One-way Flight Ticket: GBP 500-1,000 per person, depending on the season and airline

  • Airport Transfer: GBP 50-100

3. Moving and Shipping Costs

  • Shipping Container (20ft): GBP 3,000-5,000, depending on the volume of items and services included (e.g., packing, door-to-door delivery)

  • Air Freight for Essentials: GBP 500-1,000

  • Insurance: GBP 100-500, depending on the value of shipped items

4. Temporary Accommodation

  • Hotel or Airbnb: GBP 100-150 per night, estimating a 2-week stay, GBP 1,400-2,100

5. Long-term Housing

  • First Month’s Rent: GBP 1,200-2,500, depending on the neighborhood and type of accommodation

  • Security Deposit: One month’s rent (GBP 1,200-2,500)

  • Rental Agent Fees: GBP 100-300 (if applicable)

6. Settling In Costs

  • Furniture and Appliances: GBP 1,000-3,000, depending on your needs

  • Utility Setup: GBP 100-200 (installation fees for internet, electricity, etc.)

  • Groceries and Essentials: GBP 200-400 for the first month

7. Miscellaneous

  • Healthcare Insurance: GBP 50-100 per month until MSP kicks in

  • Driver’s License Exchange: CAD 31 (approx. GBP 18)

  • Social Activities and Networking: GBP 50-100 per month

Total Estimated Costs

  • Visa and Immigration: GBP 1,000-1,100 per person

  • Travel: GBP 550-1,100 per person

  • Moving and Shipping: GBP 3,600-7,000

  • Temporary Accommodation: GBP 1,400-2,100

  • Long-term Housing: GBP 2,400-5,000 (including deposit)

  • Settling In: GBP 1,300-3,600

  • Miscellaneous: GBP 100-300

Grand Total Estimate

  • Single Person: GBP 10,350 - 20,200

  • Family of Four: GBP 20,400 - 38,600 (assuming shared housing and increased visa and travel costs)

These estimates can vary based on your specific circumstances, lifestyle choices, and the time of year you move. It’s always a good idea to get quotes from various service providers and budget for unexpected expenses.
