Editor Delegation Message

Freelance Video Editor and Workflow Specialist

As a seasoned producer with a keen eye for detail, I specialize in creating captivating visual content for IT courses. My workflow is designed to streamline the editing process and ensure top-quality results. Here’s how I operate:

  • Artifacts Compilation:

Producer provides freelancer with raw footage, which producer meticulously organize into 500 artifacts based on the filename and canva flow/

  • Each artifact corresponds to a specific section of the final video which would be exported and uploaded to LMS such as Thinkific.

  • Strategic Timing:

I export the MP4 files just before handing them over to the editor from Canva as seperate files, this happens when all continuity and flow checks are done.

  • This allows producer to focus on completing the remaining sections while ensuring optimal timing for the editing phase.Editor has enough time to get through the workflow and create/agree an offer.

  • Bulk Negotiation:

Once all sections are ready, I initiate a bulk negotiation process.

  • Producer reaches out to video editors, presenting the comprehensive package of 500 artifacts.

  • Clear communication ensures that expectations align, and we’re on the same page.We do a test with a simple video the editor showcases the completion of one video in a Loom recording.

  • Agreement and Execution:

After negotiation, we agree on terms and proceed.

  • The video editor begins their magic, transforming the artifacts into a cohesive masterpiece.

Why Choose Me?

  • Efficiency: My streamlined workflow saves time and resources and give a bulk project instead of many small projects.

  • Quality: I prioritize precision and creativity and share the process with the editor.

  • Collaboration: I’m a proactive communicator, ensuring smooth collaboration with editors and share the workflow update on a guide.

Feel free to reach out for any inquiries or to discuss your project.

Remember to tailor this description to your unique skills and experiences. Best of luck on Upwork! 🎥✨
