Introduction

00:00
Hello, this is Erdem, and I'm recruiting for our Follow-Through Specialist. In this video, I'll discuss the key characteristics we're looking for when hiring for this role.

Team Culture

00:13
First and foremost, we're looking for someone who can thrive in a trust-based team environment. We're not overly competitive, nor do we operate like a close-knit family. Instead, we find a balance, fostering trust among team members.

Key Responsibilities

00:24
As a Follow-Through Specialist, your role involves ensuring that every task is completed efficiently and effectively. This means holding team members accountable and supporting them in their responsibilities.

Subscription Prompt

00:41
If you find this video helpful and want to see more content like this, please subscribe to our channel and enable notifications to stay updated with our latest videos.

Specific Requirements

00:50
We need someone proactive, detail-oriented, and capable of managing various tasks simultaneously. You will work closely with other team members, ensuring deadlines are met and quality is maintained.

Ideal Candidate Profile

01:12
We're looking for individuals who are self-motivated and can work independently while also collaborating effectively with the team. You should be comfortable handling multiple responsibilities and be able to adapt to changing priorities.

Diversity and Inclusion

02:07
We are committed to diversity and inclusion. While we don't discriminate based on gender, we strive to maintain a balanced team. This approach helps us benefit from diverse perspectives and experiences.

Communication Skills

02:31
Excellent communication skills are a must. You should be able to articulate your thoughts clearly and work effectively with team members from different backgrounds.

Task Management

03:14
Your role will involve coordinating with various stakeholders, managing schedules, and ensuring that all team members are on track with their tasks. You'll need to be organized and have a keen eye for detail.

Software Proficiency

03:45
Proficiency in tools like Google Drive, YouTube, Skillshare, and Premier Pro is essential. You'll also need to be comfortable with content creation and video editing software.

Meeting Coordination

05:05
A typical day will include coordinating and leading meetings, both with internal and external stakeholders. You'll spend about four hours in meetings and another four hours on administrative tasks.

Project Management

06:10
You'll manage workflows using a scrum and Kanban approach, ensuring all tasks are delivered on time. You'll be responsible for tracking progress and following up with team members who might be falling behind.

Accountability

07:17
You will ensure that everyone attends meetings punctually and adheres to deadlines. If necessary, you will enforce penalties to maintain discipline and productivity.

Work Schedule and Compensation

07:30
We operate year-round, and you will need to manage your vacation time effectively. Compensation starts at £400 per month for beginners, with potential earnings up to £4,000 per month for top performers.

Success Metrics

11:29
Your success will be measured by the consistency and quality of content produced by the team. Meeting deadlines and maintaining high standards will be crucial.

Personal Development

12:19
We value continuous learning and personal growth. Everyone has their limitations, but we aim to help each team member overcome them and improve their skills.

Application Process

20:11
If you believe you are the right fit for this role, please add me on LinkedIn and send a message explaining why you are suitable for this position. I prefer watching video applications over reading written ones, so feel free to create a video introduction.

Thank you for watching, and I look forward to meeting you soon.
