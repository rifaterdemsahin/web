What I Learned from Creating Videos and Why I Rebooted My Channel

Introduction

Hello everyone! Today, I want to share my journey of creating videos and the reasons behind rebooting my channel. As someone who is older and on the autistic spectrum, connecting with my audience hasn't always been easy. But through trial and error, I’ve learned valuable lessons that have shaped my approach to content creation.

The Challenges

Relating to Customers

One of the biggest challenges I faced was relating to my audience. My initial videos, where I simply shared my knowledge without addressing specific problems, didn’t resonate well with viewers. I realized that my audience wasn't just looking for information—they were looking for solutions to their problems.

Autistic Traits

Being on the autistic spectrum, I tend to focus deeply on topics that interest me. While this allows me to dive into complex subjects, it also makes it harder to understand the everyday problems my audience faces. This disconnect led to videos that were informative but not always relevant to my viewers.

The Shift in Focus

Solving Problems

I noticed that the videos gaining popularity were the ones where I solved specific problems. People were coming to my channel for solutions, not just explanations. This was a turning point. I began focusing on creating content that directly addressed common issues in the tech and IT world.

Practical Solutions

By focusing on practical solutions, I started to see a positive shift in engagement. Viewers appreciated the actionable advice and started to trust my channel as a reliable resource for solving their IT problems.

The Reboot

Why Reboot the Channel?

The decision to reboot the channel came from a need to realign my content with the needs of my audience. I wanted to build a community where viewers felt heard and where their problems were addressed effectively. The reboot was a strategic move to ensure that every piece of content added value to my viewers’ lives.

Changes Implemented

  • Customer-Centric Approach: I shifted my focus to a customer-centric approach, where every video was designed to solve a specific problem.

  • Engagement: I started interacting more with my audience, asking for feedback and understanding their pain points.

  • Content Quality: I improved the quality of my videos by investing in better equipment and editing tools to make the content more engaging and easier to follow.

Lessons Learned

Importance of Feedback

Engaging with my audience and seeking their feedback has been invaluable. It has helped me understand their needs better and create content that is more relevant and useful.

Continuous Improvement

Content creation is a journey of continuous improvement. By rebooting the channel, I committed to constantly refining my approach, learning from mistakes, and adapting to the changing needs of my audience.

Authenticity

Being authentic and sharing my unique perspective, even with its challenges, has allowed me to connect more deeply with my audience. Authenticity builds trust, and trust is crucial for a successful channel.

Conclusion

Rebooting my channel was a necessary step in aligning my content with the needs of my audience. By focusing on solving problems and engaging more with my viewers, I’ve been able to create a more valuable and trusted resource. I’m excited for this new direction and look forward to continuing this journey with all of you.

Thank you for watching and supporting the channel. If you have any questions or topics you’d like me to cover, please leave a comment below. Let’s learn and grow together!
