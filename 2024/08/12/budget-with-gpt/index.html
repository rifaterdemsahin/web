📈 Creating a Debt Payment Timeline

Step 1: Prioritize Your Debts

Given the high amounts involved, it’s crucial to prioritize your debts. Consider tackling those with the highest interest rates or those with the most significant impact on your financial stability first.

Step 2: Allocate Funds Strategically

With monthly expenses totaling £6,800, carefully allocate your remaining income to your debt payments. You may need to tighten your budget in certain areas, such as miscellaneous spending, to free up more funds for debt repayment.

Step 3: Consider Debt Restructuring

Explore options to restructure or consolidate your debts. This could potentially lower your monthly payments, making it easier to manage your overall debt load. Consult a financial advisor to explore these options.
