Embedding Content from Google Drive into Notion: A Step-by-Step Guide

As a content creator, effectively managing and sharing your raw footage and videos is crucial. One efficient way to do this is by embedding your Google Drive content directly into a Notion page. This guide will walk you through the process, ensuring your videos are easily accessible and neatly displayed.

Why Use Google Drive with Notion?

Google Drive offers a reliable storage solution for your raw footage, while Notion provides an organized workspace to manage and share your content. By embedding Google Drive videos into Notion, you can:

  • Back up your raw footage: Keep a secure copy on Google Drive.
  • Display videos seamlessly: Use Notion to create an organized, accessible library of your videos.
  • Improve workflow: Utilize tools like VVideo, which can pull content directly from Google Drive for editing.

Steps to Embed Google Drive Videos into Notion

  1. Upload Videos to Google Drive:
    - Ensure your videos are uploaded to your Google Drive account.
    - Organize them into folders for easy access.

  2. Generate Embed Code from Google Drive:
    - Open the video file in Google Drive.
    - Click on the three dots (More actions) in the top right corner and select "Embed item...".
    - Copy the iFrame code provided. The code will look something like this:
    html <iframe src="https://drive.google.com/file/d/VIDEO_ID/preview" width="640" height="480"></iframe>
    - Extract the URL within the src attribute, which contains the video ID and the preview suffix.

  3. Embed the Video in Notion:
    - Open your Notion page where you want to embed the video.
    - Type /embed and paste the URL extracted from the iFrame code.
    - Notion will generate an embedded video player, allowing you to resize and position it as needed.

  4. Organize and Annotate Your Videos in Notion:
    - Add titles, descriptions, and captions to each video for better context.
    - Use comments and tags to highlight important sections or notes.

Example of Embedding a Video

  1. Extracting the URL:
    - Suppose your iFrame code is:
    html <iframe src="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1aBcD_efGhIJK2lmNOPqr3StuVW4Xyzab/preview" width="640" height="480"></iframe>
    - The URL to use is:
    https://drive.google.com/file/d/1aBcD_efGhIJK2lmNOPqr3StuVW4Xyzab/preview

  2. Embedding in Notion:
    - Open your Notion page and type /embed.
    - Paste the URL:
    https://drive.google.com/file/d/1aBcD_efGhIJK2lmNOPqr3StuVW4Xyzab/preview
    - Resize the embedded player as needed.

Tips for a Smooth Workflow

  • Use VVideo for Editing: VVideo can pull content directly from Google Drive, streamlining your editing process.
  • Test Your Embeds: Ensure each video plays correctly in Notion before sharing the page.
  • Organize by Projects or Topics: Group related videos together for easy navigation.

Conclusion

Embedding Google Drive videos into Notion not only keeps your content organized but also makes it easily shareable and accessible. By following these steps, you can create a streamlined workflow that leverages the strengths of both platforms. Happy creating!
