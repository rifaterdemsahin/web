📋 How to Send a Proposal for VAT De-registration in the UK

Are you considering de-registering for VAT in the UK? 🚀 Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to help you navigate the process and ensure you get it right! 💡

1. Check Eligibility ✔️

Before you start, make sure you meet the criteria for de-registering. Your taxable turnover should fall below the VAT threshold, which is currently £85,000.

2. Prepare Your Proposal 📝

Gather the necessary information to include in your proposal:

  • VAT Registration Number: Your unique identifier.

  • Business Name and Address: Make sure this is accurate.

  • Reason for De-registration: Clearly state why you want to de-register.

  • Confirmation of Taxable Turnover: Provide evidence that your turnover has fallen below the threshold.

3. Complete the Online Form 🌐

You can de-register online through the HMRC website using your Government Gateway account. If you prefer, you can also use a paper form (VAT7) for this process.

4. Submit Your Proposal 📬

Once your form is complete, send it through the online portal or mail it to the address specified by HMRC.

5. Wait for Confirmation ⏳

After submission, HMRC will review your proposal. They will notify you if your de-registration is approved.
