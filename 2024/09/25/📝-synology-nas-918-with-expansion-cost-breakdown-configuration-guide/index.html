🚀 What You Should Consider:

  • Drive Brands & Capacities: You're already using a mix of Seagate IronWolf and Western Digital Red drives, which are reliable for NAS environments. For further expansion, sticking to these drive series is recommended for compatibility and performance.

  • SSD Caching: Your existing Sabrent SSDs are a great addition for cache purposes, which will significantly boost performance, especially with frequent file access.

  • Expansion with DX517: The 5-bay DX517 expansion allows you to use those additional 8TB Seagate drives, giving you substantial room for growth.

💼 Why the Synology DS918+ with DX517 Expansion?

  • Scalability: With this setup, you have 9 total drive bays (4 in the DS918+ and 5 in the DX517 expansion), allowing for a powerful storage solution.

  • Versatility: Whether you need reliable backups, media streaming, or a central file storage hub, this setup can handle it all.

  • Cache & Performance: Adding SSD caching improves the overall performance for read/write operations, especially for large files or multi-user access.
