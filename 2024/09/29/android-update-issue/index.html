It looks like your Samsung Galaxy Note 9 is encountering a loop issue while setting up a work profile, and it's prompting you to install the "Android Device Policy" app to enforce the security policies for your work email.

Here are a few steps to troubleshoot this:

  • Ensure Network Connection: Make sure you are connected to a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular) for the work profile setup to properly download and install the Android Device Policy.

  • Check for Updates: Ensure that your device is running the latest version of Android and that the Google Play Store and associated services are up to date.

  • Clear Google Play Cache:

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Google Play Store > Storage.

  • Tap on Clear Cache and Clear Data, then restart your phone.

  • Reinstall Android Device Policy:

  • Uninstall the existing version of Android Device Policy if you have already installed it.

  • Go to the Google Play Store, search for "Android Device Policy," and reinstall it.

  • Verify Email Account:

  • Make sure the email address (info@pexabo.com) is correctly added to your account under the work profile. Sometimes removing and re-adding the account can resolve issues.

  • Work Profile Reset:

  • If the problem persists, you may try to reset the work profile by going to Settings > Accounts > Remove work profile. Then re-initiate the process from the start.

Let me know if any of these steps help resolve the loop issue, or if the issue persists!
