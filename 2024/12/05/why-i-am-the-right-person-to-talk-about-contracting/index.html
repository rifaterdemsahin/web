Why I Am the Right Person to Talk About Contracting

Hello, everyone! Today, I want to share a bit about my journey and why I believe I am the right person to guide you through the world of IT contracting. This is not my favorite subject, but sometimes a little bragging is necessary to establish credibility and show you that you can trust the advice I’m offering.

My Contracting Journey

I am an IT contractor with extensive experience, having worked on contracts across five different continents. To date, I have successfully completed over 50 contracts and earned more than a million dollars in the process. Currently, my annual income ranges between $300,000 and $400,000, depending on the year and the market conditions.

My Background

I am an immigrant from Turkey who moved to the United Kingdom. Over the years, I have established companies in multiple countries, including the US, Turkey, and the UK. While I am not a native British citizen, I am on track to obtain British citizenship by the end of this year.

The Transformation

Over the last decade, I have managed to increase my income by 100 times. This transformation was not accidental; it was the result of hard work, learning, and applying the right strategies. I transitioned from being an employee to running a small business, and I did this by leveraging the power of online learning and mentorship.

Learning from the Best

My journey would not have been possible without the guidance of mentors such as Jon Summers and Eric Rooley. They provided me with the tools and insights needed to succeed in the highly competitive field of IT contracting.

Why Listen to Me?

  • Diverse Experience: I have worked in various cultural and business environments across the globe, giving me a unique perspective on contracting.

  • Proven Success: My financial success and the number of contracts I have completed speak for themselves.

  • Practical Insights: I offer practical advice that has been tested and proven in real-world scenarios.

  • Mentorship and Learning: I have benefited from great mentors and now aim to pass that knowledge on to others.

Join Me on This Journey

I am here to share the tools and strategies that helped me succeed. I want to connect with you and become a part of your journey. Whether you are just starting or looking to take your contracting career to the next level, I am here to help.

Feel free to reach out, ask questions, and engage with the content I provide. Let's embark on this journey together and achieve great success in the world of IT contracting.

Thank you for your time, and I look forward to connecting with you.
