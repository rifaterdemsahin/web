Brussels and Schools

Brussels offers a variety of schools catering to children aged 3 to 9, including international, bilingual, and local institutions. Here are some options:

1. International Schools

  • International School of Brussels (ISB)

Ages: 3–18

  • Curriculum: International Baccalaureate (IB) and inquiry-based learning.

  • Language: English.

  • Website: www.isb.be

  • British School of Brussels (BSB)

Ages: 1–18

  • Curriculum: British National Curriculum, IGCSE, and IB.

  • Language: English.

  • Website: www.britishschool.be

  • European School Brussels (EEB)

Ages: 4–18

  • Curriculum: European Baccalaureate.

  • Language: Multiple language sections (English, French, German, etc.).

  • Website: www.eeb1.eu or www.eeb2.eu

2. Bilingual Schools

  • Ecole Internationale Le Verseau

Ages: 2.5–18

  • Curriculum: French and Dutch bilingual education.

  • Language: French and Dutch.

  • Website: www.leverseau.be

  • Ecole Decroly

Ages: 3–18

  • Curriculum: French-speaking with a focus on progressive education.

  • Language: French.

  • Website: www.decroly.be

3. Local Schools

  • Dutch-Speaking Schools (Flemish Community)

Examples: Gemeentelijke Basisschool, Stedelijke Basisschool.

  • Curriculum: Flemish curriculum.

  • Language: Dutch.

  • Find schools: www.onderwijsinbrussel.be

  • French-Speaking Schools (French Community)

Examples: Ecole Fondamentale Communale, Ecole Primaire Catholique.

  • Curriculum: French curriculum.

  • Language: French.

  • Find schools: www.enseignement.be

4. Montessori Schools

  • Ecole Montessori Brussels

Ages: 2.5–12

  • Curriculum: Montessori method.

  • Language: French or bilingual (French/English).

  • Website: www.montessori-brussels.be

  • Brussels Montessori School

Ages: 2.5–12

  • Curriculum: Montessori method.

  • Language: English.

  • Website: www.brussels-montessori.be

5. Religious Schools

  • Ecole Saint-Joseph

Ages: 2.5–12

  • Curriculum: French-speaking Catholic school.

  • Language: French.

  • Website: Varies by location.

  • Ecole Sainte-Ursule

Ages: 2.5–12

  • Curriculum: French-speaking Catholic school.

  • Language: French.

  • Website: Varies by location.

Tips for Choosing a School:

  • Language: Decide if you want a monolingual, bilingual, or multilingual school.

  • Curriculum: Consider whether you prefer an international, local, or alternative curriculum.

  • Location: Proximity to your home or workplace may be important.

  • Visits: Schedule visits to schools to get a feel for the environment and teaching style.

For more information, you can contact the Brussels education authorities or visit their websites:

  • Flemish Community: www.onderwijsinbrussel.be

  • French Community: www.enseignement.be

The International School of Brussels (ISB), established in 1951, is an English-language international school located in the Brussels commune of Watermael-Boitsfort. The school offers education to over 1,350 students aged 3 to 18 from approximately 70 countries.

Campus and Facilities

ISB's 40-acre campus is situated within the Forêt de Soignes, providing a natural setting for learning. The school encourages students to utilize its sports fields and outdoor teaching facilities, promoting a holistic educational experience.
