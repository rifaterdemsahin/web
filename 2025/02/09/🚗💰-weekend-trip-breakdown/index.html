🚗💰 Weekend Trip Breakdown

Weekend Trip Plan – Milton Keynes: iFLY & Upside-Down House Adventure

🗓️ Trip Overview

• Destination: Milton Keynes

• Duration: 2 Days (Saturday – Sunday)

• Purpose: Fun family experience away from home, staying overnight in a hotel.

📍 Itinerary

Day 1: Saturday

• Morning

• Pick up the rental car via Uber from the rental app (ensure the extra driver is added if needed).

• Drive to Milton Keynes (approximately 1.5 hours from Cambridgeshire ).

• Activity: iFLY Indoor Skydiving (Duration: 2 hours)

• Afternoon

• Have lunch at a local restaurant (£50 budget).

• Check into the hotel and rest.

• Evening

• Walk around the city or visit nearby attractions.

• Dinner: Relax with a family meal (£50 budget).

Day 2: Sunday

• Morning

• Breakfast at the hotel or with groceries you brought.

• Activity: Visit the Upside-Down House (Duration: 1 hour).

• Afternoon

• Quick lunch in the city (£50 budget).

• Head back home.

• Return the rental car.

💰 Budget Breakdown

• Car Rental Deposit: £300 🔒 (refundable)

• Car Rental: £100 🚘

• Insurance: £50 🛡️

• Experiences (iFLY & Upside-Down House): £100 🤸‍♀️🏠

• Groceries: £50 🥖🧃

• Meals (Lunch & Dinner): £100

• Hotel Stay: £50 🏨

• Gas: £25 ⛽

💡 Total Estimated Cost: £475

📝 Outcomes

• Fun experience for the whole family!

• A chance to get away from your flat and spend the night at a hotel.

• Opportunity to explore Milton Keynes and enjoy unique activities.

🔧 What to Improve

• Consider taking a train for convenience with kids.

• Bring chargers and cables that fit your devices.

• Add an extra driver for flexibility.

Let me know if you want to adjust it to include train options!
