The Journey of an IT Contractor: From Employee to Global Success

Hello everyone, this is a topic that's not my favorite, but today I'm going to brag a little about myself. My name is Erdem, and I'm an IT contractor with a career that spans across the globe.

My Background and Achievements

Global Experience

I have had the privilege of contracting in five different continents, completing over 50 contracts. This extensive experience has allowed me to amass a wealth of knowledge and expertise in various IT fields.

Financial Success

Throughout my career, I've earned over a million dollars and currently make around $300,000 to $400,000 a year. Of course, these figures fluctuate depending on the year and the quality of the contracts.

Immigrant Journey

Originally from Turkey, I immigrated to the United Kingdom. I've set up companies in multiple countries, including the US, Turkey, and the UK. Despite not being a native British citizen, I anticipate obtaining British citizenship by the end of this year.

Why I Am Qualified to Guide You

Income Growth

One of the most significant achievements in my career is increasing my income by 100 times over the last decade. I transitioned from being an employee to running a successful small business.

Learning from Mentors

I've gained invaluable insights from mentors like Jon Summers and Eric Rooley. Their guidance has been instrumental in my growth and success. I believe in the power of mentorship and continuous learning, which is why I'm passionate about sharing what I've learned with others.

Sharing Knowledge

I didn't just keep this knowledge to myself. I've applied it in my career and now aim to share these insights with you. Whether you're just starting out or looking to take your career to the next level, I want to be part of your journey.

Why You Should Consider Contracting

Flexibility and Freedom

Contracting offers unparalleled flexibility. You can choose the projects you work on, the clients you work with, and often, the location from which you work. This autonomy is one of the primary reasons why many professionals choose this path.

Higher Earning Potential

As a contractor, you have the potential to earn significantly more than a traditional employee. The risks are higher, but so are the rewards. If you're willing to put in the work and continuously improve your skills, the financial benefits can be substantial.

Diverse Opportunities

Working as a contractor exposes you to a variety of projects and industries. This diversity not only makes your work more interesting but also enhances your skill set, making you more marketable.

Final Thoughts

Becoming a successful IT contractor requires dedication, continuous learning, and the willingness to take risks. My journey from an employee to a successful global contractor wasn't easy, but it was incredibly rewarding. I've learned a lot along the way, and I'm here to share those lessons with you.

If you're interested in contracting or want to learn more about how to succeed in this field, feel free to connect with me on social media. Let's embark on this journey together and achieve success in the world of IT contracting.

Thank you for listening, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
