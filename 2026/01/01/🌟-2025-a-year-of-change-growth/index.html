🌟 2025: A Year of Change & Growth

Did I get here > Future Check !

As we approach 2025, I’ve set some ambitious yet achievable goals to reshape my life and the future of my family. Here’s what I’m working on as we close out this year and move into the next.

🚀 What I Want to Achieve by the Start of 2025

1. 💸 Start Paying Off Debt

With a total debt of £74k, including personal assessment tax, loan debt, and corporate taxes, I’m taking steps to begin resolving this. Selling assets from the studio will bring in £2,000 per month for the next three months, giving me a head start.

2. 🏠 Move Out of the Studio

I’m planning to downsize the studio setup, selling off non-essential items and becoming more mobile. By cutting down unnecessary costs and optimizing my workspace, I’ll reduce overhead and increase flexibility.

3. 🔧 Resolve the Basic Gap

Filling in the gaps—financially, mentally, and organizationally—is a priority. Getting everything back on track will allow me to focus on what really matters.

4. 🇬🇧 Get Citizenship & Mira’s Passport

With both Arya and I eligible for citizenship and Mira needing a passport, I’ll ensure that our paperwork is in order by the end of the year.

5. 🎥 Weekly Video Creation Cycle

Using the tools I’ve gathered over the past few months, I’ll start releasing one video per week. My goal is to build consistency and quality in my content creation journey, contributing to my professional brand and online presence.

6. 📦 Listings for Items

As part of my plan to become more mobile, I’m creating listings for any equipment and items that no longer serve a purpose. This not only helps fund my goals but also simplifies life.

7. 🛌 Focus on Sleep, Diet, and Exercise

Mental health is a priority for my family and me. Getting into a good routine with sleep, healthy eating, and regular exercise will keep us all in a good place as we move into the new year.
